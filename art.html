I started painting with watercolor at age 5, and began drawing/sketching in the following years. Since then I've experimented with more paper/canvas techniques and styles involving ink, pencils, pastels, and markers.

While completing my undergraduate education I had the opportunity to work with new artforms including glassworking (kilncasting + coldworking) and ceramics (wheel throwing). I've enjoyed every project and every new technique. I hope to continue working with glass and ceramics in the future, when I can afford the use of necessary facilities.
